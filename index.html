Dylan Rees is a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY.
His current research combines electromicrobiology with electronic materials and device engineering.
He is also interested in sustainability, activist technologies, urban agriculture, and the application
of social science and humanities research to the practice of science and technology.
